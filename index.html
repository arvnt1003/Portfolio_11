I'm a Senior Integration & Production Support Engineer (L2/L3) with 4+ years at Tata Consultancy Services, supporting mission-critical payment gateway and webMethods middleware systems for Saudi Telecom Company (STC) — one of the largest telecom operators in the Middle East.

My expertise spans webMethods Integration Server (v10.x+), Oracle SQL, Linux/Unix administration, and Python-driven automation. I've built hands-on experience with REST/SOAP API integration and enterprise application integration (EAI) at scale — where every second of downtime translates to real business impact.

I don't just keep systems running — I engineer resilience. From automating refund lookups that eliminated two-day delays, to dashboards that reduced 60-minute manual monitoring sessions to two minutes, my work consistently turns operational pain into measurable efficiency.

I've completed two onsite rotations in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, collaborating directly with STC's technical and business teams to deliver production stability at the highest standard.
